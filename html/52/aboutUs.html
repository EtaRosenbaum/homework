All About Thyme Cafe:

Thyme Café is the sister brand of the well-known Rosemary Bagels in Lakewood, NJ, a name recognized for quality and flavor. Inspired by their family’s passion for food and community, Thyme was founded by two sisters with a vision of creating a warm, inviting space where people could enjoy fresh, flavorful meals made with care. What began as a local café has since grown into a thriving brand, with multiple locations across the United States, each one upholding the same commitment to quality and hospitality. At Thyme Café, we offer a wide range of fresh, handcrafted lunch options—from nourishing bowls and gourmet sandwiches to hearty breakfast plates served all day. We're also known for our selection of house-baked desserts, including rich pastries, cookies, and other sweet treats made daily. Whether you're stopping in for a quick coffee, a cozy brunch, or a relaxing lunch with friends, Thyme Café brings together the comfort of home-style cooking with the experience of modern café culture.
